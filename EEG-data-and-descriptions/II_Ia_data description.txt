II_Ia: This EEG data is slow cortical potentials (SCPs) recorded from a healthy subject

6 recording channels: 
Channel 1: A1-Cz (10/20 system) (A1 = left mastoid) 
Channel 2: A2-Cz 
Channel 3: 2 cm frontal of C3 
Channel 4: 2 cm parietal of C3 
Channel 5: 2 cm frontal of C4 
Channel 6: 2 cm parietal of C4 

Sampling rate: 256Hz
Each channel contains 896 samples


Data details:
II_Ia is a 2-class EEG data set and it in total contains 268 EEG trials recorded from 2 sessions: 168 trials from session 1 and
100 trials from session 2.  Each trial contains 5377 samples, in which the first column codes the class of the trial (i.e.,1/2). 
The remaining columns contain samples of the 6 EEG channels (5376 in total). In detail, it starts with 896 samples from 
channel 1 and ends with 896 samples from channel 6. 
Additionally, Class 1 contains 135 trials * 5377 samples and Class 2 contains 133 trials * 5377 samples.
